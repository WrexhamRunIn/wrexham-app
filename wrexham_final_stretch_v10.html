The AI methodology

AI compared each team’s current average points per game with the opponent’s average points per game, then adjusted slightly for home and away matches.

If the edge looked clear, AI called a win or loss. If it looked tight, AI called a draw.

Then AI converted those picks into standard football points — 3 for a win, 1 for a draw, 0 for a loss — and rolled them into the projected final table.
